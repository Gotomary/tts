Welcome to the Text-to-Speech System

User Guide

1. Choose Voice: This allows you to select the kind of voice available on the system. You can select either a female voice or a male voice with multiple accents too.

2. Write Message: This allows you to input the text you want to covert into speech. .

3. : This icon serves as volume control. The slider in front of it allows you to control your volume level.

4. : This icon serves as rate control. The slider in front of it allows you to regulate how fast you want your audio output to be.

5. : This icon serves as pitch control. The slider in front of it allows you to regulate how deep you the sound of the audio output to be.

6. : This icon allows you to play your audio output. It can also be used after you might have paused or stop your audio output.

7. : This icon allows you to pause your audio output. After which, the Play icon can be used to continue the audio output.

8. : This icon allows you to stop or put an end to your audio output. After which, the Play icon can be used to restart the audio output all over again.
